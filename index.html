What we offer

Pet Shop

We have a diverse selection of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, fish, and small mammals. We even have a charming old turtle named Gaddis! We take pride in giving "recycled" dogs and cats a second chance with loving new owners.

Kennel

Our kennel provides comfortable and safe boarding for your dog, cat, or bird while you travel. With a capacity of 10 pets, we ensure a personal and calm environment. We have a clear rate of €20 per day for boarding.

Our Story

VIAPets began as a simple pet shop in Horsens Denmark, founded by retired football manager Bob Oldenuff. Originally known as "Pets," our store provided a range of pets to bring joy and companionship into people’s lives. During the pandemic, we saw a surge of new pet owners, and as life returned to normal, we found a new way to support our customers by offering top-quality kennel services for their furry friends.

With this expansion, we became VIAPets, named to reflect our commitment to treating every pet as a “VIA”—a Very Important Animal. We are proud to provide both a welcoming place for pet adoption and a trusted home-away-from-home for pets while their owners are away.

Our unique approach includes offering “recycled” pets—loving dogs and cats who are ready for a fresh start with a new family. This environmentally conscious approach resonates with our community and helps each pet find the perfect forever home.

VIAPets continues to grow as a combined pet shop and kennel, dedicated to supporting pet lovers with all their pet care needs.
